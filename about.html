FAQ'S...


1. How do I book an appointment?

Please refer to our reservations page to view more information on how to book an afternoon tea event.


2. What is the limited number of guests per group?

Although we encourage the motto of "The more, the merrier!", we do have a limit of 25 guests per group to ensure an intimate and exclusive experience for all.


3. Can I swap out certain ingredients due to allergens?

We do have a separate vegan menu available upon request. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions or allergies when booking your appointment.
