Réservations...

Our esteemed high tea hosting service operates from Monday to Saturday, including public holidays, between 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM. Guests are invited to indulge in an all-you-can-eat experience for R500 per person, with the freedom to create their own 3-tier compositions.

There is no limit to group size, and each group will be attended by one butler per five guests, ensuring a personalized and luxurious experience. To book your high tea experience, please follow the guide below.

1. Choose a date

Before contact, please ensure that you have a suitable date in mind

2. Contact us

Please refer to our contact page to book a date and time. This can be done via telephone or E-Mail.

3. Guest invitations...

Verify the number of guests in your booking. Physical addresses of guests will be required for physical copies of invitations to be sent out.

4. Payment

The relevant financial details will be shared with the primary booking holder depending on the chosen payment type. The payment must be completed first for physical invitations to be shared.
