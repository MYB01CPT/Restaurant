Nouvelle Apéritifs...

A delicious assortment derived from vintage England, Mer D'étoiles is pleased to announce that tea sandwiches have been added to our list of entrées!

Offered breads include classic white, brown, sourdough, rye, honey and oats, ciabatta, and our original sliced baguette!

Visitors are encouraged to try our delectable fillings: cucumber and cream cheese, egg salad, smoked salmon and cream cheese, tuna mayonnaise, chicken mayonnaise, pastrami, or our unique addition of carpaccio~

Our wonderful breads are freshly baked and all meats are prepared carefully. These tea sandwiches are exquisite if we do say so ourselves... We recommend cucumber and cream cheese alongside a cup of fragrant pomegranate tea!
